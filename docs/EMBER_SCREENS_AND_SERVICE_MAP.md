# EMBER — Screen Plan & Service Map

### From journey to buildable spec

> Vocabulary is locked from prior docs: **Ember** (app & companion), **your Ember** (the being, in temperaments Seer/Wright/Root/Ferry), **the Inner Country** (world), **the Witness** (sensing), **the Circle** (your team of 4), **the Woken** (the six support companions), **Tending** (living well), **the Hush** (atrophy), **Seasons**, **the Commons** (social).

-----

# PART A — THE SCREEN PLAN

*Each screen: **purpose · key elements · the principle it honors · states.** Grouped by flow and mapped to journey phases.*

## A · Onboarding & Activation (Phase 0–1)

**A1 — Welcome (the promise)**
One beautiful frame, one sentence. · Hero art of a dim ember in a vast country; the line; one button. · *Aesthetic-Usability + honest external trigger.* · States: first-launch only.

**A2 — How it works (3-card primer)**
Set the conceptual model before any choice. · Three cards: *you live, it grows · you never tap to feed · it can dim but never die.* · *Norman conceptual model + progressive disclosure.* · Skippable after first view.

**A3 — Choose your temperament**
The starter choice as a mirror, not a difficulty. · 1–2 temperaments at MVP (Seer/Root), each with a one-line self-portrait; “this is how you already meet the world.” · *Hick’s Law (tiny choice set).* · States: locked-in after pick; changeable later in F4.

**A4 — Name your Ember**
First investment; personalization. · Text field, a default suggestion, the ember reacting to the name. · *Eyal Investment (small, loads next session).*

**A5 — Connect the Witness (sensing + privacy)**
The trust gate. · Plain-language explanation of what’s sensed (breath/HRV via camera, sleep & movement via Health/wearables) and why; granular toggles; “your record is yours” promise; link to the Ledger. · *Nielsen: user control & honest status; trust before data.* · States: full grant / partial grant (degrade gracefully) / decline (still usable with self-report).

**A6 — First breath — the aha** ⭐
The activation moment. · A single guided sensed breath; a valley visibly warms in real time; ends on the ember catching. · *Immediate feedback (closes Gulf of Evaluation) + Peak-End engineered to end on warmth + lowest-cost first Action (B=MAP).* · Success metric: lit valley within ~20s.

## B · The Daily Core (Phase 2)

**B1 — Home: the Inner Country**
The living world is the home screen — *not* a dashboard. · Your Ember in its country; current season & brightness shown as landscape, never a number; lit/dim regions; one gentle entry to tend. · *Visibility of status rendered as world; minimalism (anti-Whoop).* · States: bright / mid / Hush (greying); seasonal skins.

**B2 — Your Ember (the relationship)**
Tap the Ember. · Its temperament, growth across imagination/resilience/tooling (shown organically, not as bars), the current want/need beat, a line of voice. · *Conceptual model deepens; the bond.* · States: thriving / dim / mid-storm.

**B3 — Today: the Witness reflection**
The honest reflection that refuses to be a verdict. · “What your living made today” — how breath/rest/movement/attention/connection showed up as light; the Hearthkeeper’s overnight dream-harvest (a new idea at dawn). *No 0–100 score, no red.* · *Eyal 2nd-order: reward = recognition, not judgment.*

**B4 — Tend: the practice library (the fuel)**
Practices that wake the Circle. · Breath, stillness, movement, rest, reach-out prompts; each tagged to the Woken it stirs; guided sessions. · *Content is fuel, not the product; success ≠ time-in-content.* · States: suggested-for-today / browse / in-session.

**B5 — The Kindle (session close)**
The designed END of every visit. · What lit, your Ember’s state, a single warm line; a clean exit. · *Peak-End: every session ends on warmth.*

## C · World & Crew (Phase 4)

**C1 — The Circle (team of 4)**
Your one Ember + triad of three. · The four, their domains and current state; rotate the triad; coverage hint (your blind-spot vector). · *Investment + meaningful team-building (refuses gacha).*

**C2 — A Woken (companion detail)**
Depth per support being. · The practice that wakes it, its gift (tool granted), its shadow-when-dim, its small arc. · *Character depth → attachment.*

**C3 — Wake a companion (recruit flow)**
Recruit by living, never by chance. · Sustain the practice → the companion stirs → wakes; a small ceremony. · *Eyal Investment; explicit refusal of blind-box scarcity.*

**C4 — Seasons (your life, recorded)**
The stored value and the arc. · Timeline of kindling and Hush, storms survived; “ever since” framing; exportable. · *Investment as a record genuinely the user’s own.*

**C5 — Regions (explore the country)** *(optional / can fold into B1)*
Zoomed view of lit/dim regions tied to life areas. · *Goal-gradient, used gently.*

## D · Hush, Return & Storm (Phase 3) ⭐ *the differentiator*

**D1 — The Hush (Home, quiet state)**
Not a separate screen — Home greys honestly. · Color drained at edges, crew quiet, tools asleep; the ember still glowing at center. · *Blame prevention; honest status without judgment.*

**D2 — The Return: welcome back** ⭐ *the most important screen in the app*
After any lapse, re-entry is relief, never debt. · “Welcome back. Your ember held.” The ember visibly still alight; one warm breath to begin again; **no streak-broken, no catch-up, no guilt.** · *Peak-End on return; the structural anti-Dealer proof.* · States: short lapse / long lapse (warmer, never heavier).

**D3 — The Storm (hard-day support)**
When life is hard (sensed dysregulation / poor recovery / withdrawal), the crew rallies. · Supportive not alarming; a designed peak of being-held; **a real floor** (see safety path in Part B). · *Service recovery; care, not gamified comfort.*

## E · The Commons (Phase 5)

**E1 — The Commons (neighboring Hearths)**
Tend alongside others; encouragement, never ranking. · Hearths of people you know / your cohort; send warmth; **no leaderboards, no vanity metrics.** · *Reward of the tribe, made co-regulatory.*

**E2 — A Hearth (visit & encourage)**
See another’s country (shared view only). · Send warmth, co-regulate, share a season. · *Social proof for support, not comparison.*

**E3 — Send warmth (co-regulate)**
The interaction. · A warm, low-cost gesture; the Kin stirs on both sides. · *Refuses status/envy mechanics.*

## F · Inversion & System (Phase 6 + cross-cutting)

**F1 — “Go live, I’ll be here” (the inversion)**
The screen that tells you to leave. · An emergent prompt: go breathe/train/connect for real; the country thrives *because* you put the phone down. · *Indistractable made literal; north-star = lives tended, not minutes captured.*

**F2 — The Witness controls (the Ledger)**
Privacy, sensing, data ownership. · Granular sensing toggles; export/delete your record; “your record is yours.” · *User control & data dignity.*

**F3 — Triggers (notification design)**
Only the user’s own scheduled traction prompts. · No app-timed FOMO; the user sets when (if ever) Ember nudges. · *Indistractable: traction prompts only.*

**F4 — You (profile & long arc)**
Identity and the long view. · Your temperament, your seasons, change-your-temperament path. · *Mastery & reflection.*

# PART B — THE SERVICE MAP

## B.1 — Backstage systems (named architecture)

- **The Witness** — sensing & ingestion: camera PPG (breath/HRV), HealthKit/wearable APIs (sleep, RHR, movement), with graceful degradation to self-report.
- **The Loom** — the world-state engine: turns sensed signals into the Inner Country’s brightness, region-lighting, season, and Hush. Runs continuously + an **overnight pass** (season turning, the dream-harvest).
- **The Kindling** — the practice/content engine: the library that maps each practice to the Woken it wakes.
- **Circle logic** — personalization: companion state, growth vectors, want/need beats, recruit eligibility.
- **The Commons service** — social backend: Hearths, co-regulation, warmth (cooperative only — no ranking primitives even exist).
- **The Ledger** — data ownership, privacy, export/delete. The record of how you lived belongs to the user.
- **Studio / Live-ops** — seasons, events, content cadence; and the business funnel (the app is the front door; membership → cohort is the business).
- **Care path** — the safety floor (escalation to real resources when signals suggest genuine crisis; see B.4).

## B.2 — The service blueprint

|Phase                    |User action                   |Frontstage (screens)|Backstage                             |Systems                   |**Facilitator guardrail**                 |
|-------------------------|------------------------------|--------------------|--------------------------------------|--------------------------|------------------------------------------|
|**Arrival**              |sees promise, opens           |A1–A2               |render hero, set expectation          |Studio (acquisition)      |honest promise — *no fake urgency*        |
|**First session**        |picks, names, grants, breathes|A3–A6               |sense first breath → light valley     |Witness, Loom, Ledger     |reward = recognition — *no spike*         |
|**Daily loop**           |lives; checks in              |B1–B5               |sense → weave world → reflect         |Witness, Loom, Kindling   |status as world — *no 0–100 verdict*      |
|**Lapse / Hush / Return**|drifts, returns               |D1–D2               |detect lapse → grey gently → welcome  |Loom                      |**welcome, ember held — *no streak/debt***|
|**Storm**                |struggles                     |D3                  |detect distress → rally crew → *floor*|Loom, Care path           |hold, don’t gamify; escalate if real      |
|**Deepening**            |drafts crew, lives a season   |C1–C4               |wake companions, accrue history       |Circle logic, Loom, Ledger|stored value is *theirs* — *no lock-in*   |
|**Commons**              |tends alongside               |E1–E3               |match Hearths, route warmth           |Commons                   |co-regulation — *no leaderboard*          |
|**Inversion**            |puts phone down               |F1                  |celebrate off-app living              |Loom, Studio (metrics)    |metric = lives tended — *not minutes*     |

**Line of interaction** sits above Frontstage; **line of visibility** between Frontstage and Backstage; **line of internal interaction** above Systems. The Facilitator guardrail is the row that must never be value-engineered away under monetization pressure.

## B.3 — Critical service-recovery moments

1. **The Return (D2)** — the make-or-break service moment. Prototype first. A returning user must feel *relief*, never debt. This single recovery design is what proves the whole service is a Facilitator.
1. **The Storm (D3)** — sensed hard days are met with the crew and a designed sense of being held — *with a real floor beneath it.*

## B.4 — The safety floor (non-negotiable)

A service that senses distress carries a duty of care. When the Witness + behavior suggest genuine crisis (not a normal Hush), the service must route to **real human resources and crisis support**, plainly and immediately — never gamified comfort, never the crew alone. This path is always reachable, lives outside the fiction when it needs to, and is designed with professional input. (This is also the clearest expression of the whole thesis: care over engagement, always.)

## OPEN BUILD DECISIONS

1. **Home = the world, or a hub?** I’ve specced Home *as* the Inner Country (the bold, differentiated choice). A safer hub-with-world-inside is the alternative.
1. **The Witness on MVP** — camera PPG only (max reach, more novel) vs. HealthKit-first (reliable, familiar)? Journey doc leaned phone-first; the Loom should be source-agnostic regardless.
1. **The Return’s range** — how different should short-lapse vs. long-lapse re-entry feel? My lean: longer lapse → *warmer*, never heavier.
1. **Care path design partner** — who do we bring in to build B.4 responsibly before any launch?
