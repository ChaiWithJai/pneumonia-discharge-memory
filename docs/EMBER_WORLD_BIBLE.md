# EMBER — App Concept Treatment

### The product, named — and positioned against the right prior art

-----

## THE NAME

**Recommended: *Ember.***

It names the one thing nothing else in the category has: the indestructible core, the vow that *it can never fully go out.* It’s warm, short, memorable, and the entire arc is literally an ember growing into a flame growing into a fire. The brand name *is* the product’s deepest promise — and the anti-guilt stance of the whole thing.

If we take *Ember*, the lexicon collapses cleanly: the being you tend is **your Ember**; it begins as an ember and grows; it can dim but never die. (This removes the earlier “Spark vs. Ember” naming collision — the companion and the core become one word.)

|Rank |Name        |Why                                                   |Flag                                            |
|-----|------------|------------------------------------------------------|------------------------------------------------|
|**1**|**Ember**   |the vow as the name; ember→flame→fire arc             |trademark-crowded (mug, JS framework) — clear it|
|2    |**Tend**    |the entire thesis as one verb; modern, app-store clean|crowded (dental, home security)                 |
|3    |**Kindling**|active, the feeding itself; less crowded              |slightly long                                   |
|4    |**Hearth**  |warmth, home, the kept fire                           |check availability                              |

*The companion/world lexicon below adapts to whichever name wins.*

## POSITIONING LINE

> **Ember is a living companion that grows from how you actually live.** It’s the first well-being app where the thing you tend is your own intelligence — sensed from your body, not self-reported from a checklist — and it can dim through seasons but never die.

**Comparable shorthand (for fast pitching):** *Finch, if the pet were your own mind and it grew from your physiology instead of a to-do list — with the soul of* Soul *and the sensing of Whoop.*

## THE CONCEPT

*What the app actually does.*

You open Ember and **choose your temperament** — not a difficulty, a mirror. From then on, **you don’t play; you live, and the Witness notices.** Your breath, your sleep, your training, your moments of stillness and connection are sensed (from the phone and from any wearable you already own), and the Inner Country answers — a valley warms, a color returns, your Ember grows a little more imaginative, resilient, capable.

Through real practice you **wake the Circle**: a true breath wakes the Bellows, a real rep wakes the Strider, reaching for a friend wakes the Kin. Each lends your Ember a tool. The crew you assemble is a literal portrait of how you’ve been living.

When you lapse, **the Hush** sets in — color drains, recent tools sleep, storms land harder — but the ember holds. Always. The seasons turn. You return, and the warmth comes back as relief, never as a debt. Over time, in the **Commons**, you tend alongside others, and the strength that comes from not being alone is real and mechanical.

There is no level to beat. The win — re-winnable every season — is the moment you stop tending out of dread and start tending because the country is yours and you love it.

## THE PRIOR-ART MAP

*The centerpiece. Every layer has a category leader. We name it, take its proven pattern, and refuse its shadow.*

|Layer                              |Prior art that owns it                          |What we TAKE                                                                                                                                        |What we REFUSE                                                                                                                                               |
|-----------------------------------|------------------------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
|**Care-companion as motivation**   |**Finch**                                       |the “do it for something that depends on you” reframe; the warm, shame-free tone; a personalized practice library                                   |self-reported task checklist as the input; in-app currency & accessories; a generic pet instead of *you*; no real atrophy or arc                             |
|**Atrophy with a living thing**    |**Forest**                                      |a living thing whose state visibly tracks your behavior; *felt* stakes                                                                              |the **death/guilt** — Forest kills your tree to punish you. The Ember can never die.                                                                         |
|**Physiological sensing**          |**Whoop / Oura**                                |multi-signal, baseline-relative sensing of how you actually lived (HRV, RHR, respiration, sleep, movement); ingest their data so we need no hardware|the **0–100 verdict** and the red “your body is broken” judgment; quantified-self anxiety. We render signal as a *living world*, not a score.                |
|**Collection + party**             |**Pokémon (Sleep/GO)** · **Habitica**           |a meaningful team you build (1+3); the social party/cohort                                                                                          |Pokémon’s completism & gacha; Habitica’s extrinsic XP/gold and “failing hurts you and your party” punishment                                                 |
|**Practice content**               |**Calm · Headspace · Waking Up · Insight Timer**|guided breath/meditation/movement as the *fuel* that wakes the Circle                                                                               |content-as-the-product; streak pressure; the success-equals-churn trap. Content is fuel, not the destination.                                                |
|**AI companion / personification** |**Replika · Character.AI · Tolan**              |a personified companion with personality and a real bond                                                                                            |the bond-via-endless-chat and engagement maximization. Our bond is to your *interior state*; sometimes the companion’s job is to make you put the phone down.|
|**Connected collectible / culture**|**Tamagotchi (Uni) · Pop Mart / Labubu**        |portability, emotional projection, belonging, the carried object                                                                                    |neglect-death (Tamagotchi) and blind-box scarcity/gacha (Pop Mart)                                                                                           |

**The pattern of refusals is the product.** Every leader monetizes a form of pressure — guilt (Forest), judgment (Whoop’s red), completism (Pokémon), punishment (Habitica), engagement (Replika), scarcity (Pop Mart). Ember’s entire fence is built by refusing each one. That’s not just ethics; it’s the only un-clonable thing, because every incumbent’s business model depends on the pressure we won’t use.

## THE CATEGORY WE’RE CREATING

Not a habit tracker, not a wearable, not a meditation app, not a chatbot. **Ember is the first *interior-state companion*: a being bonded to how you actually are, that grows by your living and survives by a promise.** The honest one-line category claim:

> *Conscious gaming, finally shipped — the companion-collectible the market is chasing as an “AI Labubu,” built on real interior state instead of scarcity.*

-----

## MVP — the smallest version that proves the soul

The Pixar test from the lattice was: *does a stranger feel the ember before anyone explains it?* The MVP exists to answer exactly that.

- **One temperament** to start (not four) — pick the most universal; likely the Root or the Seer.
- **The Witness reads three signals**: breath/HRV (phone camera PPG or Apple Health), sleep, and movement. No proprietary hardware.
- **The Inner Country brightens and dims** in response — the core feeling, fully realized, even if the world is small.
- **The Hush as gentle seasons**, and the ember that visibly never dies — the single most testable promise.
- **One or two of the Woken** (Bellows + Strider), to prove the wake-by-practice loop.
- **No Commons yet, no collection, no monetization.** Those come after the feeling is proven.

The MVP is a success if a tester who knows nothing about *atman* or *tejas* says *“I didn’t want to let it go dark”* — and then, *“but it was okay when I came back.”*

1. **The name** — Ember (rec), or Tend / Kindling / Hearth? This decides whether the companion is “your Ember” or “your Spark.”
1. **Hardware stance** — phone-only (camera PPG, max reach) vs. ingest-wearables (deeper signal, smaller TAM) vs. our own carried object (the “anti-phone” play). MVP says phone-only; the long game may differ.
1. **The Commons timing** — does the social/cohort layer ship in v1 (moat early, more to build) or v2 (prove the solo soul first)?
